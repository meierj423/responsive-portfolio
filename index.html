About Me

Hello! My name is Jack Meier and I am currently enrolled in UCSD Extension's Coding Bootcamp. I served in the U.S. Navy for three years as an Intelligence Officer prior to the beginning of the bootcamp. My longtime fascination with technology and computers has sparked an interest in me beginning a career in software development.

I was born in Ann Arbor, Michigan and moved a number of times before leaving for college. I attended New Mexico Military Institute in Rosewell, New Mexico as a prep year before attending the U.S. Naval Academy. I earned my B.S in Mathematics from the academy and commissioned as an officer in May 2017.

My hobbies include going on adventures with my wife whether it is traveling or finding a new place to eat in San Diego. I also enjoy running, watching sports, reading, cooking/baking, bowling, playing boardgames, and taking our puppy, Ollie, out for walks.
